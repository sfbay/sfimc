Identity

Naming Concept: SFIMC

A naming approach built around the central "i" ‚Äî the letter that joins, defines, and carries the meaning.

The Concept

sfimc

"sf" (San Francisco) + "i" (Independent) + "mc" (Media Coalition)
The "i" joins the halves while carrying the core meaning

The "i" Paradox

Visually lighter ‚Äî recedes, bridges, connects
Conceptually central ‚Äî independent, information, the reason the coalition exists

Like a hinge: small, but holds everything together. The quiet letter that carries the meaning.

Visual Treatment Options

sfimc Lighter weight "i" ‚Äî recedes but joins
sfimc Accent color "i" ‚Äî stands out, defines (Roger Black approach)
SFiMC Mixed case ‚Äî emphasizes the joining role
